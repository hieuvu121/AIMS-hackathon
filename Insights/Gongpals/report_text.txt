Modern Slavery Compliance Lite Report - Development Prompt
PROJECT OVERVIEW
Create a web-based lite report dashboard for modern slavery compliance analysis focusing on the 2 largest sectors by weight in the AIMS dataset over a 5-year period (2020-2025) for demonstration and comparison purposes.
REQUIRED FEATURES & COMPONENTS

Data Scope Summary Section


Display summary statistics of the analyzed dataset
Show date range, number of companies, sectors covered
Include data collection methodology overview


Overall Compliance Rate Dashboard


Primary Display: Overall compliance percentage as a clickable metric
Interactive Feature: When clicked, show a heatmap/matrix visualization

Rows: Different sectors
Color intensity: Compliance rates (darker = lower compliance)
Include hover tooltips with exact percentages




Key Findings Section


Summary of main insights from the analysis
Highlight critical compliance gaps
Statistical summaries of sector performance


Average Disclosure Score Visualization


Interactive chart showing average disclosure scores (out of 100%)
Sector Filter: Dropdown/toggle to filter by specific sectors
Display scores for each weighted category:

Structure and operation (10% weight)
Supply chain (15% weight)
Modern slavery risks in operations and supply chains (30% weight)
Due diligence and remediation processes (25% weight)
Effective assessment (20% weight)




Policy vs Action Analysis


Chart showing "Percentage of companies with remediation policies but zero action taken"
Filter by sectors
Include breakdown by company size if data available


Minimum Requirement Analysis


Display "Percentage of companies that do not meet Level 1 bare minimum requirements"
Filter by sector
Trend Visualization: Line chart (2020-2025)

X-axis: Years
Y-axis: Percentage not meeting requirements
Multiple lines: One per sector
Interactive legend to show/hide sectors




Further Findings - Detailed Compliance Analysis

SCORING METHODOLOGY
For each requirement, calculate compliance using this 2-level system:

Each criterion has multiple sections with 2 levels each
Calculate mean percentage for Level 1 and Level 2 compliance
If company meets Level 2, they automatically meet Level 1
Single-level sections are mandatory

REQUIRED COMPLIANCE METRICS (each with Level 1/Level 2 granularity):

Organizational Coverage

Score based on coverage of linked organizations
50% weight per level


Organizational Structure Description

Organization and supply chain structure
Supply chain understanding
Direct and indirect suppliers
50% weight per level


Remediation Policies Score

Policy comprehensiveness and implementation
50% weight per level


Risk Management Processes

Assessment procedures for modern slavery risks
Supply chain risk management
50% weight per level


Modern Slavery Policies

Policy existence and quality
Implementation framework
50% weight per level


Employee Training Programs

Training coverage and effectiveness
Modern slavery awareness programs
50% weight per level


Effectiveness Assessment

Due diligence process evaluation
Action effectiveness measurement
Prevention and response mechanisms
50% weight per level
