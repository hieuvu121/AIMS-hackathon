About Us

Learn about our mission to eliminate modern slavery from supply chains worldwide through data-driven insights and collaborative action.

Our Mission

Light Carriers is dedicated to eliminating modern slavery from global supply chains through comprehensive data analysis, transparent reporting, and collaborative action. We believe that by shining a light on modern slavery reporting practices, we can drive meaningful change and protect vulnerable workers worldwide.

Our mission is to provide organizations with the tools, insights, and support they need to identify, prevent, and eliminate modern slavery from their supply chains. We work with businesses, NGOs, governments, and other stakeholders to create a more ethical and transparent global economy.

Our Approach

We use data-driven insights to assess modern slavery reporting across business sectors, identifying trends, gaps, and best practices. Our comprehensive methodology evaluates policy development, implementation effectiveness, and transparency levels to provide actionable recommendations for improvement.

Our approach combines quantitative analysis with qualitative assessment, incorporating stakeholder feedback and expert opinions to ensure comprehensive and balanced evaluation. We focus on practical solutions and real-world impact, helping organizations implement effective modern slavery prevention and remediation measures.

Our Team

Our team brings together experts in human rights, supply chain management, data analysis, and policy development. We have extensive experience working with multinational corporations, NGOs, and government agencies to address modern slavery and human trafficking issues.

Our diverse team includes researchers, analysts, policy experts, and field practitioners who work collaboratively to develop innovative solutions and drive meaningful change. We are committed to continuous learning and improvement, staying current with evolving best practices and emerging challenges in the fight against modern slavery.

Our Impact

Since our founding, we have helped hundreds of organizations improve their modern slavery reporting and compliance efforts. Our data and insights have been used by businesses, investors, and policymakers to make informed decisions and drive positive change.

We have contributed to the development of industry standards, best practice guidelines, and policy recommendations that have improved modern slavery prevention and remediation efforts worldwide. Our work has been recognized by international organizations and industry leaders for its impact and innovation.

Our Values

We are committed to transparency, accountability, and collaboration in all our work. We believe that addressing modern slavery requires a collective effort and that all stakeholders have a role to play in creating a more ethical and sustainable global economy.

Our values guide everything we do, from our research methodology to our stakeholder engagement approach. We are committed to continuous improvement, learning from our experiences and adapting our methods to better serve our mission and the communities we work with.
